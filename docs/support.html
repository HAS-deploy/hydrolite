HydroLite — Support

Last updated: 2026-04-17

How do I undo a log?

Tap the Undo button in the top right of the Today tab — it removes the most recent log. You can also swipe-to-delete any row in History.

How do I restore a purchase?

Settings → Restore purchases. Use the same Apple ID that bought the Lifetime Unlock.

My reminders aren't firing.

Check iOS Settings → HydroLite → Notifications. Also check Settings → Reminders → Quiet hours to make sure the reminder time isn't inside your quiet window.

Does HydroLite track calories or electrolyte content?

No. HydroLite is intentionally simple: water volume and an optional electrolyte-drink toggle. It does not track calories, sodium, or other nutrients.

Is HydroLite a medical app?

No. It's a hydration logger. It doesn't diagnose, treat, monitor, or manage any health condition. Hydration targets are user-controlled general defaults, not medical recommendations.
